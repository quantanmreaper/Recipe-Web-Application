Dum Veg Biryani

User123 Date: 10/09/2024 (120 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee and fry onions till golden brown.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and spices. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer the rice and cook on dum for 20 minutes.
